Facts

The United Nation Classifies
Kenya

Kenya’s water crisis is acute, with a low water replenishment rate leading to only 56% of the population having clean water access. The decline in sanitation coverage exacerbates health issues, with 80% of hospital visits being preventable diseases linked to water and hygiene. Rural and urban areas alike face sanitation challenges, impacting schools and increasing vulnerability to natural disasters..
