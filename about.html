About Us

A Beacon of Hope for Lambwe

The Horace C. Tucker Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of the Lambwe community in Kenya, Africa. It focuses on providing sustainable clean water, healthcare, and education, while also empowering local structures and engaging stakeholders to enhance service delivery and livelihood security within the community

Helped Fund 6 Projects in Lambwe community,
Benefiting Over 100 People.

What We Do

Services
Water & education

Ensuring sustainable clean water for the Lambwe community and promoting educational programs, empowering individuals and contributing to long-term well-being.

Community well-being

We work to strengthen local structures, enabling them to fulfill their mandates effectively, and Engage stakeholders in efficient service delivery.

Healthcare Advocacy

By partnering with existing health facilities, we advocate for primary healthcare services. Their approach emphasizes collaboration and community participation
