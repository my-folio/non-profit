About US

Who are we

The Horace C Tucker Foundation, based in Simi Valley, CA, is dedicated to improving the lives of the Lambwe community in Kenya. It focuses on providing sustainable clean water, primary healthcare, and educational programs. The foundation also works to enhance local structures and stakeholder engagement. It addresses Kenya's water scarcity and sanitation issues, with all donation proceeds directly benefiting Lambwe.

The United Nation Classifies Kenya

Kenya faces severe water scarcity with low replenishment rates and only 56% of the population having access to safe water. Poor sanitation contributes to preventable diseases, accounting for 80% of hospital visits. Half of the population lacks adequate sanitation, with worse conditions in rural areas and urban slums.

Furthermore, more than three-quarters of Kenya remains vulnerable to disasters, particularly floods, droughts, and cholera.
